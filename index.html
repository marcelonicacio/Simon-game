Instructions

  1. Press the button POWER on the front of the game unit, then press the button PLAY to start the game.
  2. A color will flash. Repeat the sequence by pressing the same color.
  3. The game will continue. After each light sequence that you repeat successfully, a light will be added to the end.
  4. When you don't complete a sequence successfully, the SIMON game will display a message "NO!".
  5. Try to beat your high score!
